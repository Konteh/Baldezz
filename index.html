about me

Who is Sarjo Baldeh.

I am a 20 year old Gambian young woman. I started Photography at the age of 15. I fell in love with the Art of Photography from the very first day I took pictures of students at starfish. everyone was happy and that out so much joy in my heart. It told a story that impacted people’s lives. It has become a burning passion for me. Photography is a way I use to express my feelings, to share with the world the beauty it has even when times are hard. What makes me more happy is when my pictures puts a smile on people’s faces. Photography makes me experience life at another level. I got to learn a lot from people and it is what I love doing. I had the courage to venture into a male dominated career because I believe in equal opportunities for both men and women. I think it is one of the ways I can add light and beauty to this world. When people see me. With the photographs I take and will take, I hope to make the world a better place.
